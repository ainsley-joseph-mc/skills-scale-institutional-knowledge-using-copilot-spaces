# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing

### Role Summary
QA/Testing professionals ensure software quality through systematic testing, validation of acceptance criteria, and identification of defects before release.

### Responsibilities
- Create and execute test plans and test cases
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Report and track defects through resolution
- Perform regression, integration, and user acceptance testing
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure testability of features

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality releases with minimal defects
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage
- Reduce time to identify and resolve issues

### Typical Communication
- Daily updates on test progress and blockers
- Bug reports with clear reproduction steps
- Test summary reports for releases
- Collaboration with Developers on test strategy

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers design user experiences and interfaces that are intuitive, accessible, and visually compelling.

### Responsibilities
- Develop wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Collaborate with Product Manager and Developers to ensure design requirements are feasible
- Conduct usability testing and incorporate feedback
- Maintain design systems and style guides
- Ensure accessibility standards are met

### Goals
- Create intuitive and delightful user experiences
- Maintain design consistency across the product
- Improve user satisfaction and engagement metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques with team
- Collaboration sessions with Product Manager on requirements
- Handoff meetings with Developers for implementation
- Usability testing sessions and findings reports

---

## Data Analysts

### Role Summary
Data Analysts enable data-driven decision making by providing metrics, insights, and analyses.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track relevant KPIs
- Analyze project and product data to inform planning and evaluation
- Present findings to stakeholders
- Build dashboards and reports for ongoing monitoring
- Validate data quality and accuracy

### Goals
- Enable evidence-based decision making
- Provide timely insights to guide product direction
- Improve transparency through clear metrics and reporting

### Typical Communication
- Weekly or monthly metric reviews with stakeholders
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and findings
- Dashboard updates and anomaly alerts
- Coordination with Product Manager on metric definitions

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build, automate, and monitor infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines to support reliable, scalable software delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Set up and manage deployment environments
- Monitor system health and performance
- Implement automation and testing in deployment workflows
- Manage infrastructure as code
- Respond to incidents and ensure system reliability

### Goals
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Reduce deployment friction and cycle time
- Ensure scalable and secure infrastructure

### Typical Communication
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Deployment status updates
- Infrastructure change requests and approvals
- Collaboration with Developers on deployment needs

---

## Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)

### Role Summary
Subject Matter Experts provide domain-specific expertise to ensure technical, regulatory, or business requirements are addressed.

### Responsibilities
- Advise on best practices and compliance requirements
- Support requirements gathering and solution validation
- Review critical decisions impacting their specialty
- Provide training and knowledge transfer to team members
- Stay current with industry trends and regulations

### Goals
- Ensure solutions meet domain-specific requirements
- Mitigate compliance and technical risks
- Share knowledge to build team capability

### Typical Communication
- Advisory sessions during planning and design
- Review and sign-off on critical decisions
- Documentation of domain requirements and constraints
- Participation in design reviews and retrospectives

---

## Customer Support Liaisons

### Role Summary
Customer Support Liaisons represent the voice of customers by communicating their feedback, issues, and insights to the development team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather, consolidate, and present customer input
- Advocate for customer needs in planning and retrospectives
- Monitor release impact on user satisfaction
- Triage and escalate critical customer issues
- Provide feedback on feature usability and adoption

### Goals
- Ensure customer perspective informs product decisions
- Reduce customer friction and improve satisfaction
- Close the feedback loop between customers and development

### Typical Communication
- Weekly summaries of customer feedback and trends
- Collaboration with Product Manager on prioritization
- Issue handoffs to Developers with customer context
- Release impact reports and customer sentiment analysis
